Honesty

Honesty is important because it helps us build trusting relationships and promote credibility and transparency in our social interactions. How to improve honesty? Always tell the truth, take responsibility for your actions and decisions, do not lie or deceive others, respect confidentiality and privacy.

Solidarity

It's about collaboration and mutual support between people. Solidarity is important because it helps us overcome adversity and face life's challenges together. How to strengthen solidarity? Helping others in times of need, being empathetic and supportive of others' struggles, working as a team to achieve common goals, and advocating for social justice and equality.

Responsability

Accountability is important because it helps us develop a strong work ethic and be responsible and engaged citizens. How can we improve accountability? By respecting our obligations and commitments, admitting mistakes and learning from them, taking action to correct the damage caused and becoming a responsible and ethical leader.

Empathy

Empathy is important because it helps us develop healthy and meaningful relationships with others, promote understanding and compassion, and build more inclusive and just communities. How to improve empathy? Through active listening, asking open-ended questions, putting yourself in the other person's shoes, validating others' feelings, practicing everyday situations, volunteering, and reading literature/films that explore different perspectives.
