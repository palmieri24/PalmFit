FAQs

How can I sign up?
Joining the team is simple! Create your new account by confirming your email and enter all the data to be able to structure your plan. You will receive an email immediately to complete the payment and start the journey! Please note that it will take up to 24 hours to structure the plan.
How will we track the results?
The results will be graphed and used to evaluate progress and how to proceed.
I'm new, is this good for beginners?
The training is entirely tailored to you and your goals. So if you're new to the gym, I'll help you with your personalized program. Choose light weights to start and increase gradually. You will find the video with explanation of each exercise in the sheet. If you have doubts about the executions, send us the video via email, so we can help you!
Will it be the same every month?
No, every month is different. This will involve weight gain, a change in exercises and changes in your eating plan which will be constantly updated.
Can I train from home?
If you wish, we can customize the plan so you can train at home! I advise you to have at least short elastic bands and 2 dumbbells, to be replaced if necessary with 2 bottles of water.
Is the service subscription based?
YES. PalmFit app is a subscription-based app, so you will be automatically charged every month, three months, or twelve months, depending on the subscription you choose. But there are no lock-in contracts, so you can cancel at any time. However, you must cancel 72 hours before the end of your subscription to avoid being charged for the next subscription period.
How can I unsubscribe?
To cancel your subscription you must email us at least 3 days notice so that you are not charged again. Please note that when you cancel all plans the previous program will be removed from your account.
